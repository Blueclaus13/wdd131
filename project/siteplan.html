Paws World - Site Plan

Site Name:

This is the name of the community center an Association without lucrative purposes which rescue dogs from the street.

Site Purpose:

The main purpose is to design a platform where the association can share the dogs in adoption, in that way, we can increase their visibility and the adoption requests. Furthermore, the association needs to have a medium where to communicate fundraising activities or merchandise.

Scenarios

  • What requirements do I need to adopt?

  • How can I support the assosiation?

  • Where can I meet the dogs before deciding?

Color schema

  • Dark Brown: #5f3711 (primary color) - Text and other elements.

  • Light Brown: #efbc98 (secondary color) - backgrounds.

  • Cream #eb9d8d- An auxiliary color for contrast.

  • Light Cream #f6f6e2 - backgrounds.

  • Black: #000000 - text and other elements.

  • White: #ffffff - text and backgrounds.
